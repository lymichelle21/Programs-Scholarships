Scholarships

BHW Scholarship
The BHW Scholarship is offered to female students pursuing degrees in science, mathematics, technology, or engineering. It is valued at $3,000.

Science Ambassador Scholarship
The Science Ambassador Scholarship is a no-essay scholarship for female STEM students. You must create a 3 minute YouTube video on a STEM subject of your choosing. This scholarship covers full tuition.

NACME Scholarships
NACME has a variety of sponsored scholarships for minorities in engineering. Scholarships range from semester to full year and go up to $5,000.

Against The Grain Artistic Scholarship
The Against The Grain Artistic Scholarship is a scholarship for Asian American college students who are pursuing a degree in the performing, visual arts, journalism and/or mass communications. It is valued at $1,000.

Fontana Transport Inc. Scholars Program
This scholarship is offered to underrepresented, first-time, and low-income students who are majoring in transportation, math, science, engineering, architecture, environmental design, PreMed, psychology, Spanish language/literature. It is valued at $5,000.

CHCI Institute Scholarship Program
The CHCI Institute Scholarship Program offers a scholarship for social science majors of Hispanic or Latino heritage. The award amount ranges from 1.5k to 5k.

The National Science Foundation Graduate Fellowship
"The National Science Foundation Graduate Fellowship provides research grants to graduate level students pursuing their degrees in science, technology, engineering or mathematics. Students must be enrolled at an accredited college or university, and special focus is given to minority students including Native Americans."

ACS Scholars Program
The ACS Scholars Program offers a $5,000 scholarship to underrepresented minority students who want to enter the fields of chemistry or chemistry-related fields. This includes African American, Hispanic, Pacific Islander (excluding Asian) or American Indian high school seniors or college freshman, sophomores, or juniors.

The Heinlein Society Scholarship Program
The Heinlein Society Scholarship Program is designed to assist female undergraduate students who are majoring in math, engineering, or biological or physical sciences. Three scholarships, each for $3,000, are awarded each year.

The Art Career Project Scholarships
The Art Career Project offers a $2,000 Scholarship for Minority Artists, created to support underrepresented students in art majors. The Art Career Project also offers a variety of other scholarships.

Catching The Dream Program
Catching The Dream is a program that offers scholarships to Native college students who demonstrate promise in their chosen major. Applicants must be a quarter or more degree Native American. The scholarships is valued at $5,000.
